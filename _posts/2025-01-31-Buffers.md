    <td></td>

* casein is vey difficult to solubilize. You can do it with a magnetic stirrer. If casein is slowly added to the buffer while stirring you can solubilize it.  
* alternatively: casein  resuspended in an alkaline solution and then the neutralization and addition of NaCl results in a casein solution in TBS. The key is to neutralize with HCl very gradually. Good luck!
* skimmed milk contains a lot of casein and is easily solubilized
